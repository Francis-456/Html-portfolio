The Best Movies According to Yiga Francis

My top 3 Movies of all Time

APOCALYPTO

Apocalypto portrays the hero's journey of a young man
Jaguar Paw, a late Mesoamerican hunter and his fellow tribesmen
who are captured by an invading force

WELCOME TO EDEN

A group of young adults attend a party on a remote island,
but the tempting paradise they find waiting for them
dangerous secrets and entrapments

A TRIBE CALLED JUDAH

A Tribe Called Judah tells the story of a resilient single mother
raising five sons from different fathers
